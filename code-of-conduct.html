Code of Conduct

Our Commitment

At Lemonca Ecosinoptic, we are committed to creating a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for all members of our environmental community. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for behavior within our app, community forums, and all related spaces.

Our Mission

We believe that environmental protection and sustainability are global challenges that require collaboration, respect, and understanding across diverse communities. Our mission is to foster a supportive environment where everyone can contribute to making our planet greener and more sustainable.

Expected Behavior

All participants in the Lemonca Ecosinoptic community are expected to:

  • Be Respectful: Treat all community members with kindness, respect, and dignity, regardless of their background, beliefs, or level of environmental knowledge.
  • Be Inclusive: Welcome and support people of all backgrounds, including but not limited to race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, gender identity, sexual orientation, age, ability, and socioeconomic status.
  • Be Collaborative: Work together constructively to achieve environmental goals and support each other's efforts.
  • Be Honest: Share accurate information about environmental issues and your own environmental practices.
  • Be Constructive: Provide helpful, supportive feedback and avoid criticism that doesn't contribute to positive change.
  • Be Patient: Understand that everyone is at different stages in their environmental journey and learning process.

Environmental Responsibility

As a community focused on environmental protection, we expect all members to:

  • Practice What We Preach: Strive to live sustainably and reduce your environmental impact.
  • Share Knowledge: Help educate others about environmental issues and sustainable practices.
  • Support Local Initiatives: Participate in and promote local environmental campaigns and initiatives.
  • Stay Informed: Keep up with current environmental issues and share accurate, science-based information.
  • Lead by Example: Demonstrate sustainable practices in your daily life and encourage others to do the same.

Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable and will not be tolerated:

  • Harassment: Any form of harassment, bullying, or intimidation of other community members.
  • Discrimination: Discriminatory language or behavior based on race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, gender, sexual orientation, age, ability, or any other personal characteristic.
  • Misinformation: Deliberately spreading false or misleading information about environmental issues.
  • Spam: Excessive posting of promotional content or irrelevant information.
  • Privacy Violations: Sharing personal information about other community members without their consent.
  • Disruptive Behavior: Behavior that interferes with the community's ability to function effectively.
  • Environmental Harm: Promoting or engaging in activities that cause significant environmental damage.

When a violation is reported, our team will:

Community Guidelines for Environmental Discussions

When discussing environmental topics, please:

  • Use Evidence-Based Information: Support your arguments with reliable, scientific sources when possible.
  • Be Open to Learning: Be willing to learn from others and consider different perspectives.
  • Avoid Extremism: While passion for environmental issues is encouraged, avoid extreme or harmful rhetoric.
  • Focus on Solutions: When discussing problems, also consider and share potential solutions.
  • Respect Different Approaches: Understand that there are many valid ways to contribute to environmental protection.

Updates to This Code

This Code of Conduct may be updated periodically to reflect the evolving needs of our community. All updates will be communicated to community members, and the most current version will always be available in the app and on our website.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this Code of Conduct or need clarification on any points, please don't hesitate to contact us:

  • Email: conduct@lemonca.pro
  • In-App Support: Available through the app's help section
  • Community Moderators: Available in community forums

Thank You

Thank you for being part of the Lemonca Ecosinoptic community and for helping us create a positive, inclusive environment where we can all work together for a greener future. Your commitment to respectful behavior and environmental responsibility makes our community stronger.
