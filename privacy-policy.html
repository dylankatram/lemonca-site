Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Lemonca Ecosinoptic ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

By using Lemonca Ecosinoptic, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our app.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and password when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Name, profile picture, and preferences you choose to share
  • Contact Information: Email address for communications and support

Usage Information

  • App Usage: How you interact with our app, features used, and time spent
  • Campaign Participation: Campaigns you join, challenges completed, and environmental activities
  • Environmental Data: Air quality preferences, location data (with consent), and environmental impact tracking

Device Information

  • Device Details: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Location Data: GPS location (only with your explicit consent) for environmental data and local campaigns
  • Network Information: IP address and connection type for service optimization

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

  • Environmental Data Providers: We may share location data with environmental data providers to deliver air quality and environmental information
  • Analytics Services: Anonymous usage data may be shared with analytics providers to improve our services
  • Cloud Services: Your data is stored securely on cloud infrastructure providers

Legal Requirements

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Respond to legal requests and court orders
  • Protect our rights, property, and safety
  • Prevent fraud and security threats

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction, subject to the same privacy protections.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication measures
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Employee Training: Privacy and security training for all staff
